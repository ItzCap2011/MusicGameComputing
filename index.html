The Art of War

How to Play

Notes fall down four lanes toward the glowing targets. Press the matching key the instant a note lands on its target.

SPACE start / restart  ·  ESC pause  ·  PERFECT and GOOD hits wound the king; a MISS lets his attack land on you. Glowing gold notes are special royal strikes — extra punishing if missed!

Tall notes are holds: press as the head reaches the target, keep the key down while the trail drains, then release at the end. Topple the King to face the Queen, who fights almost entirely with holds.
